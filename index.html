Welcome!

Welcome to Misty Kingdom! Established in 2022 by the Mattathil Family, we provide a cozy homestay experience in Munnar. With spacious rooms, stunning mountain views, and various activities, we make sure every guest feels at home. Come and enjoy a peaceful retreat with us!

Exciting Adventures Await You

At Misty Kingdom, we provide a variety of exciting activities to enhance your stay, including:

Jeep Safaris

Join an exciting Jeep safari at Misty Kingdom! Explore Munnar's stunning landscapes and enjoy thrilling off-road adventures with breathtaking views of lush hills and wildlife

Amusement Park Visits

Experience a day of fun at our Amusement Park! Enjoy thrilling rides, entertaining games, and create unforgettable memories for all ages

Nature Walks

Explore the beautiful surroundings on guided nature walks, where you can discover local flora and fauna in a serene setting.

Fish Spa Treatments

Indulge in our rejuvenating Fish Spa Treatments! Experience the unique sensation of tiny fish gently exfoliating your feet, leaving your skin refreshed and revitalized.

Hot Air Balloon Rides

Soar high with our Hot Air Balloon Rides! Enjoy breathtaking aerial views of Munnar's stunning landscapes as you glide peacefully through the sky.

Zip Lining

Experience the thrill of Zip Lining at Misty Kingdom! Glide through the treetops and enjoy exhilarating views of the lush landscapes below.
